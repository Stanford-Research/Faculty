Execution Strategy

  1. Start with the first 10–15 Purple profiles. Read one recent paper/lab page before outreach.

  3. Message for a precise pointer, not favors. Ask “which paper/angle should I study first?”
  4. Only after reply, ask whether a follow-up or introduction is appropriate.

Funding Interpretation

  1. High means active CS/AI/HAI/lab relevance, not guaranteed scholarship.
  2. Medium means adjacent research funding is possible if your project fits.
  3. Low/None means do not treat this as a funding path.
  4. For MS funding, your strongest play is research fit + assistantship/RA opportunity + external scholarships.
