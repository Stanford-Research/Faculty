Outreach operating plan

The plan is not to ask powerful people for favors. The plan is to become legible as a serious builder-researcher in their exact research ecosystem.

Week 1 · Study & map

Read 2–3 papers from Landay/Bernstein/HAI/HCI. Extract vocabulary, research questions, and interface principles.

Week 2 · Public presence

Comment intelligently on HCI/HAI posts. No admissions language. No “please guide me.” Only research-level observations.

Week 3 · Artifact

Publish a 4–6 page research note connecting UbiFit/Bloom, reflective interfaces, and Detoxia’s offline-first behavioral intelligence.

Week 4 · Network expansion

Reach PhD students, alumni, and recent faculty around the same topic. Ask for paper pointers, not referrals.
